Sales
Tire Sales Representative – U.S. Market
United States (Flexible within major tire markets)

We are seeking an experienced Tire Sales Representative with proven expertise in the U.S. tire industry to drive sales growth by developing new accounts and strengthening the distribution network with wholesalers and retailers.

Details & requirements

Overview: We are seeking an experienced and highly motivated Tire Sales Representative with proven expertise in the U.S. tire industry. The ideal candidate will be responsible for driving sales growth by developing new accounts, expanding distribution networks, and strengthening relationships with wholesalers, regional distributors, and tire retail shops.

Key Responsabilities:

  • • Identify, approach, and secure new business with tire distributors, wholesalers, and retail stores across the U.S.
  • • Develop and maintain strong, long-term relationships with existing customers.
  • • Achieve and exceed sales targets through strategic account management and market development.
  • • Conduct regular market research to identify trends, competitor activity, and customer needs.
  • • Provide product knowledge, technical support, and training to clients as needed.
  • • Represent the company at trade shows, industry events, and client meetings.

Qualifications:

  • • Sales experience in the U.S. market.
  • • Strong existing network with tire distributors, wholesalers, and retail outlets.
  • • Deep understanding of the U.S. tire industry, including PCR, LTR, TBR, and specialty tires.
  • • Excellent negotiation, communication, and relationship-building skills.
  • • Self-motivated, results-driven, and able to work independently with minimal supervision.
  • • Willingness to travel frequently within the U.S. to meet customers and develop markets.

What we offer:

  • • Competitive base salary plus commission.
  • • Performance-based incentives..
  • • Opportunities for career growth in a rapidly expanding company.
  • • Supportive and collaborative work environment.

How to Apply: Interested candidates should send their resume and a brief cover letter highlighting relevant tire sales experience to christina@landscapetires.com with the subject line: “Application – Tire Sales Representative”.

A highly strategic and results-driven Brand Manager/Marketing Leader specializing in the U.S. market. Proven ability to conduct in-depth competitor analysis, manage multi-channel promotional campaigns (digital, trade shows, social media), and collaborate cross-functionally to ensure consistent brand alignment across all product and sales touchpoints while optimizing ROI and channel development.

Details & requirements

Key Responsabilities:

  • 1. Brand Strategy & Positioning
  • 2. Marketing & Promotion
  • 3. Market Operations & Channel Development
  • 4. Brand Alignment & Execution
  • 5. Research & Analysis

Qualifications:

  • • Bachelor’s degree or above in Marketing, International Business, Brand Management, or related fields.
  • • 3+ years of proven experience in U.S. market brand promotion or marketing operations, with strong understanding of Positioning Theory.
  • • Solid background in market research, data-driven analysis, and execution of marketing strategies.
  • • Familiarity with U.S. marketing channels (Google Ads, Meta Ads, industry publications, trade shows, distributor collaborations).
  • • Excellent communication and cross-cultural collaboration skills; proficiency in English as a working language.
  • • Prior experience in the automotive, tire, industrial products, or durable goods industries is a strong plus.

What we offer:

  • • Competitive salary and performance-based bonus..
