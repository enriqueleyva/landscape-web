Wear-Resistant Compound

  • Advanced polymer composite formulation achieves a balance of wear resistance, toughness, and high-temperature performance
  • Nano-scale uniform dispersion technology enhances rubber reinforcement and minimizes localized stress concentration.

Toughening Process

  • 1. Independently controlled temperature and pressure vulcanization process prevents over- or under-curing, ensuring structural consistency of the tire body.
  • 2. AI detection and precision control system with multi-point sensors enables real-time monitoring and automatic adjustment of production parameters.

Impact-Resistant Structure

  • 1. High-modulus cord fabric increases carcass rigidity, disperses impact stress, and prevents structural fatigue and tearing.
  • 2. BPOT adaptive crown technology automatically adjusts the tire’s contact shape according to road pressure, optimizing multi-point pressure distribution and reducing localized wear.

Traction Tread Design

  • 1. Self-cleaning tread pattern design automatically removes mud, snow, and stones to maintain safe traction.
  • 2. Multi-curvature tread block technology adapts to varying road conditions and load distributions, enhancing wear resistance across multiple terrains.

Reinforced Framework

  • 1. Ultra-high-strength steel belts enhance carcass load-bearing capability.
  • 2. Low-denier, high-strength cord fabric improves impact resistance of the tire body.
