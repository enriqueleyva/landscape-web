Program Durability & Wear

We’re inviting Uber drivers to join our official Tire Durability Testing Program — to verify real-world wear performance and help us build our Durability Wall of Fame.

Who We’re Looking For

  • Active Uber or Lyft drivers
  • High annual mileage (city and highway driving preferred)
  • Willing to share regular feedback and tire condition reports

Program Details

  • Install our designated tire models for on-road testing
  • Provide periodic feedback and photos
  • Submit a short user comment including the keyword “durable”
  • Selected drivers will be featured on our website and partner stores as part of the Durability Wall of Fame

What You’ll Receive

  • Free or discounted tire installation
  • Regular tire inspections and technical support
  • Official exposure with your photo and testimonial
  • Brand gift pack and priority access to future campaigns
