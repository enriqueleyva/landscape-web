Innovation and Heritage

At the heart of every Landscape tire lies our proprietary system of innovations — Aegis High-Durability Tech™ — a comprehensive technology platform that balances wear-resistance, toughness, and performance through precision engineering.

  • Hybrid Compound 2.0:an advanced polymer blend that improves both toughness and wear resistance.
  • High-Dispersion Mixing Technology: ensures uniform distribution of rubber components, reducing localized tread wear.
  • Temperature-Pressure Separation Vulcanization : prevents lifespan variations by precisely controlling curing conditions.
  • Ultra-Uniform Intelligent Manufacturing: maintains consistent tire structure and automatically adjusts production parameters for optimal balance.
  • Reinforced Casing Technologyenhances impact and tear resistance for longer durability.
  • BPOT Adaptive Crown Technology: optimizes multi-point pressure distribution, minimizing localized wear and extending tread life.
  • Self-Cleaning Tread Design automatically expels mud, snow, and stones to maintain strong grip and safety.
  • Multi-Curvature Tread Block Technology: increases traction and wear balance across diverse terrains.
  • High-Tenacity Polyester Cord:strengthens carcass structure and improves tire stability.
  • Open-Type UT Steel Belt: enhances bonding between steel and rubber for superior structural integrity.
